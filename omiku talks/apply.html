before you apply

who are we
looking for?

We are looking for someone 18 or older who genuinely gets people. You have lived through real highs and lows, and you carry that experience with wisdom and empathy. You are the person your friends come to when life gets hard, naturally giving advice and helping others find their way.

You can de-escalate a situation, hold space for hard conversations, and stay grounded when things get tense. But you also know how to have fun and make people feel at ease. Professional when it matters, real always.

If that is you, we would love to hear from you.

Takes approx. 10–15 min to complete

Podcast & Server
Staff Application

Thank you for your interest in joining our podcast or becoming a staff member on our server. Please answer the questions below honestly and thoughtfully. We're looking for kind, motivated individuals who can give meaningful advice and are open to growth. We will review your application and get back to you within 1–7 business days.

Section 1: Basic Information
01What name do you go by?
02How did you hear about this podcast/server?
03How old are you?
04What are your pronouns?
05Which role are you applying for?
06How much time can you realistically commit each week?
Section 2: Personality & Values
07In your own words, what does being a kind person mean?
08What motivates you to stay consistent and show up, even on hard days?
09What kind of energy do you bring into a group or community?
Section 3: Advice & Growth
10What is one piece of advice you strongly believe in and why?
11If someone felt lost or overwhelmed in life, what would you say to them?
12What is something you are currently working on improving about yourself?
Section 4: Scenario Questions
13Someone in the server feels left out and ignored. What would you do or say?
14Two members are arguing and it's affecting the community. How would you handle it?
15A guest or member says something offensive or controversial. What would you do?
16You made a mistake publicly (on the podcast or in the server). What would you do next?
Section 5: Final Question
17Why do you want this opportunity, and what impact would you hope to make?
Section 6: Agreement & Consent
✿ Application submitted, thank you! We'll be in touch within 1–7 business days.
