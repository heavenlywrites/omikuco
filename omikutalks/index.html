✦ about the show

Welcome to omiku talks, where every episode feels like a real late night conversation. Each episode dives into topics like heartbreak, first loves, friendships, family, and everyday life. I share stories, give advice to people who reach out, and talk through things in an honest, easy way. Sometimes it’s just me, sometimes I bring my friends on, but it’s always real, unfiltered, and relatable. Think of it as free therapy, just without the license.
